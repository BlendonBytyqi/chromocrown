About CHROMOCROWN

CHROMOCROWN was founded in 2025 in Prishtina, the capital city of Kosovo, with a clear vision: to bring precision, elegance, and trust into the world of luxury timepieces.

The idea behind CHROMOCROWN was born from a deep appreciation for craftsmanship and the belief that a watch is more than just a tool for telling time. It is a symbol of personality, success, and legacy. With this philosophy in mind, the brand was created to offer watches that combine innovation, tradition, and timeless design.

From the beginning, CHROMOCROWN focused on offering carefully selected watches from globally respected brands such as Casio, Rolex, and Patek Philippe.
